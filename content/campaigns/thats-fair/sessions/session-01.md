- Arturo leaves the party in the middle of the night
- Nenkelde, Emen, and Thava see a job advert for adventuring from someone named Tink. They meet him at his workshop at the Grand Mechanicum to inquire about the job.
- Tink tells them they have to meet the benefactor - a friend of Tink’s uncle named Gundren Rockseeker - at the Driftwood Tavern, after receiving a letter from Gundren
- They meet the benefactor along with his bodyguard - Sildar Winterhall - to discuss the job which is to deliver supplies to his mining company in Phandalin. It should take about 3.5 days to arrive at Phandalin.
- Gundren promises to deliver something to Tink that will help Tink with his ultimate goal which is to create a new unified theory of the universe which incorporates magic upon completion of the job.
- The party agrees to the job. They wake up to a note from Gundren saying he needed to leave early and the party sets off.
- On the Triboar trail, they see two dead horses belonging to Gundren and Sildar and are ambushed by goblin bandits
- After fighting off the goblin bandits, the party no longer have a way to Phandalin but notice goblin tracks heading north in hopes of finding Gundren and Sildar.
- They follow the tracks to a cave and encounter wolves and a Bugbear. They defeat them but see no sign of Gundren and Sildar.
