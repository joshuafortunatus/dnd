Story:

- After defeating the Red Brands’ ambush, they interrogate one of the gang members who gives up that Glasstaff, who heads the Red Brands, was hired by the Spider and is hiding at the manor.  
- They stayed at Sister Garaele’s who gives them a sidequest to encounter a banshee.  
- Townmaster Harbin Wester castigates the party for causing a raucous. The party becomes suspicious and asks Linene about Wester. She says to look for impropriety at his house.  
- Thava and Tink break into Wester’s house but an alarm goes off and alerts Wester. He walks to his house with Emen and Nenkelde and luckily Wester, although suspicious, walks away without finding Thava and Tink who had hidden themselves in a closet.  
- The party moves onto Alderleaf Farm and meets Kellen and Carp, who found the entrance to the cave.   
- Then they move toward the cave, take out two guards, and enter to expect 10-12 people including an Eyemonster, undead, bugbears, and Glasstaff. As Emen moves across a bridge, the party’s minds begin to tingle.
