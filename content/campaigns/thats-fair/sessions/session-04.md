Story:

- The group defeated a [Nothic](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/17092-nothic?srsltid=AfmBOoqSpCorFtwSJ29fWTftADcQyS2er9eEkWsWIXsWwd-3dnLKBSR4) and looted the cavern.  
- They venture deeper into the cavern and find a wizard’s alchemical workshop. A rat seems to be spying on them, and it disappears when attacked by Tink.   
- They find out that Glasstaff the wizard is brewing invisibility.  
- They find a journal by Urmon, an adventuring dwarf. In it, he explains the history of Lostmine, the forge of spells in Wave Echo Cave where magic items were forged by fandelder’s pact of Dwarves, humans, and gnomes 500+ years ago. A magic mace named lightbringer was commissioned by priests of Lafhander. Disaster struck when they were attacked by orcs and human mercenary wizards. It all might be tied to what Gundren Found.  
- In a desk, they find a letter to Lord Albrek \- who is revealed to be Glasstaff \- in cahoots with The Spider.  
- They leave the workshop, realizing that Glasstaff recently left. They run into the Red Bands and fight them off. They must choose to continue pursuing and risk another fight, or turn back and potentially lose their best chance at capturing Glasstaff.
