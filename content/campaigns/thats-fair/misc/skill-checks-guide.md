| Skill | Usage Rank | Role Playing | Combat |
| :---: | :---: | ----- | ----- |
| Acrobatics | 7 | Navigate narrow ledges, tightrope walk across chasms, or balance on precarious surfaces during exploration Perform impressive physical feats to entertain crowds, win contests, or gain entry to performer's guilds Squeeze through tight spaces like ventilation shafts, collapsed passages, or prison bars | Escape grapples by twisting free from an enemy's grip Maintain footing on slippery, icy, or unstable terrain during battle Tumble through enemy spaces to reach priority targets or avoid opportunity attacks |
| Animal Handling | 16 | Calm aggressive animals to pass through their territory safely or gain them as temporary allies Train mounts for combat, teach animals tricks, or establish long-term bonds with animal companions Read animal body language to determine if they're diseased, cursed, or under magical control | Control your mount during combat to execute complex maneuvers or prevent it from bolting Calm an enemy's mount or beast companion, potentially dismounting riders or removing combatants Redirect aggressive animals toward enemies or away from vulnerable party members |
| Arcana | 9 | Identify magical items, decipher arcane symbols, or understand the purpose of ritual circles Recall knowledge about magical creatures, planar entities, and their weaknesses or behaviors Detect and analyze magical traps, wards, or illusions before the party triggers them | Recognize enemy spells being cast to warn allies what's coming (e.g., "That's Fireball—spread out\!") Identify the magical nature of enemy abilities to determine damage types and resistances Determine which magical effect is sustaining an enemy (concentration spell, glyph, artifact) to target the source |
| Athletics | 6 | Break down doors, bust open locked chests, or force open barred gates when stealth isn't an option Intimidate through physical displays like bending iron bars, crushing objects, or arm-wrestling contests Climb sheer surfaces, scale walls during escapes, or traverse difficult vertical terrain without equipment | Shove enemies off ledges, into hazards, or away from vulnerable allies Grapple opponents to lock down dangerous spellcasters or prevent enemies from fleeing Break free from restraints, grapples, or being swallowed by creatures |
| Deception | 8 | Lie convincingly to guards, officials, or enemies to talk your way out of trouble Create believable disguises or cover stories to infiltrate enemy organizations Bluff during negotiations to make your position seem stronger than it actually is | Feint or create distractions mid-combat to mislead enemies about your true target or intentions Impersonate allies or enemy commanders to cause confusion in enemy ranks Bluff about reinforcements, hidden traps, or the party's remaining resources to cause enemies to hesitate |
| History | 12 | Recall ancient prophecies, historical events, or past wars that provide context for current quests Identify valuable historical artifacts, artwork, or relics that others might overlook Recognize heraldry, noble lineages, or organizational symbols to navigate political situations | Remember tactical information about famous battles fought in the current location to exploit terrain Recall historical weaknesses or defeats of legendary enemies you're currently facing Identify ancient war constructs, undead generals, or historical warriors and know their signature tactics |
| Insight | 4 | Detect lies, half-truths, or omissions during negotiations, interrogations, or social encounters Read body language and emotional states to identify who's trustworthy versus who's planning betrayal Sense when NPCs are charmed, possessed, or replaced by noticing behavioral inconsistencies | Predict enemy tactics by reading their body language and fighting stance Find out what weaknesses or vulnerabilities an enemy possesses Identify which foes are likely to flee or surrender based on their morale and demeanor |
| Intimidation | 10 | Extract information through threats, forcing NPCs to reveal secrets they'd otherwise protect Establish dominance in social situations to gain respect from criminals, mercenaries, or warriors Discourage potential threats from attacking by making clear the consequences of hostility | Force weaker enemies to flee or surrender without further bloodshed Create openings by making enemies hesitate, flinch, or fight defensively Break enemy morale after killing their leader or strongest member with a terrifying display |
| Investigation | 2 | Search crime scenes for hidden clues, secret compartments, or overlooked evidence Deduce trap mechanisms, puzzle solutions, or the purpose of mysterious machinery through logical analysis Find concealed doors, hidden passages, or structural weaknesses in walls and floors | Identify environmental hazards to exploit (chandeliers to drop, weak floors to collapse, explosive barrels) Analyze enemy formations to spot tactical weaknesses or identify who's the leader/support Locate hidden enemies, invisible foes, or camouflaged creatures through careful observation |
| Medicine | 17 | Stabilize dying NPCs or party members without using spell slots or healing potions Diagnose diseases, poisons, or curses to determine proper treatment or necessary spells Perform field surgery, set broken bones, or treat wounds during short rests for faster recovery | Stabilize fallen allies at 0 HP without using actions for healing spells or potions Identify poison or disease effects on allies mid-combat to inform treatment priorities Determine if "dead" enemies are truly deceased or merely unconscious/playing dead |
| Nature | 14 | Identify safe plants for food/medicine versus poisonous varieties during wilderness survival Predict weather patterns to prepare for storms, find shelter, or plan optimal travel times Track animals or recognize territorial signs to avoid dangerous beasts or find prey | Recall beast and plant creature vulnerabilities, resistances, and special abilities Exploit natural terrain features like quicksand, thorny growth, or unstable ground against enemies Predict animal behavior to calm, provoke, or redirect wild creatures during encounters |
| Perception | 1 | Spot hidden enemies, lurking ambushes, or concealed observers before they strike Notice small details others miss: bloodstains, drag marks, recent disturbances in dust or foliage Hear distant sounds like approaching guards, rushing water, or collapsing structures as early warnings  | Win initiative by noticing hostile intent before combat officially starts Spot hidden or invisible enemies during their turn to alert allies and negate advantage Notice environmental changes mid-combat like reinforcements arriving or traps being activated |
| Performance | 15 | Entertain crowds to gather information, earn coin, or create diversions for party members Impersonate specific individuals convincingly using acting skills rather than just disguise Win contests, gain entry to performance venues, or earn favor with artistic patrons  | Distract enemies with flashy displays, drawing attention away from more vulnerable allies Demoralize foes through taunting performances that highlight their failures or fears Inspire allies mid-battle through war chants, battle cries, or heroic displays (outside Bardic Inspiration)  |
| Persuasion | 5 | Negotiate favorable deals, lower prices, or gain concessions from reasonable NPCs Convince NPCs to help the party, share information, or provide resources voluntarily Recruit allies, build alliances, or turn neutral parties into supporters of your cause | Convince intelligent enemies to stand down, switch sides, or reveal information mid-combat Rally frightened NPCs or wavering allies to continue fighting instead of fleeing Negotiate temporary truces or ceasefires when combat becomes disadvantageous |
| Religion | 18 | Identify holy symbols, divine artifacts, or religious texts to determine deity affiliations Recall religious customs, holy days, or proper temple etiquette to gain favor with faithful NPCs Understand undead creation methods, celestial hierarchies, or fiendish pacts | Recognize undead types and their specific vulnerabilities (radiant damage, turning immunity, etc.) Identify divine spells being cast by enemy clerics or paladins Recall celestial or fiendish weaknesses, resistances, and behavioral patterns during extraplanar encounters |
| Sleight of Hand | 11 | Pickpocket keys, documents, or coin purses from guards and NPCs without detection Plant evidence, stolen goods, or tracking devices on unsuspecting targets Conceal weapons, tools, or contraband during inspections or pat-downs | Disarm traps mid-combat without taking your full action Steal spell components, holy symbols, or material focuses from enemy casters Quick-draw hidden backup weapons when disarmed or make feints to create openings |
| Stealth | 3 | Scout ahead to identify enemy positions, trap locations, and patrol patterns before the party commits Tail suspects through crowded streets or wilderness without being detected Hide evidence, conceal your presence at crime scenes, or eavesdrop on private conversations | Hide between turns to gain advantage on attacks and force enemies to waste actions searching Sneak into flanking positions to gain advantage and help set up ambushes Reposition without drawing attention to reach vulnerable targets like archers or spellcasters |
| Survival | 13 | Track creatures through wilderness by following footprints, broken branches, or other signs Forage for food and water, find shelter, and navigate without getting lost in hostile terrain Predict natural dangers like avalanches, flash floods, or animal migration patterns | Identify the most defensible terrain positions for your party before enemies arrive Track fleeing enemies to prevent escape or follow them to their base Navigate difficult terrain efficiently during combat (forests, swamps, mountains) while enemies struggle |
