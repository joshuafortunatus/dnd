Upon achieving your honorific at the Grand Mechanicum, you are eager to get out into the world to test your theories on the nature of magic. However, you have not quite found the correct opportunity to do so yet. So you take a position working in the auxiliary workshop at the school. One morning as you are tinkering about in the shop, a letter arrives addressed to you.

Dear Sir,

I hope this letter finds you well. My name is Gundren Rockseeker. I am an old friend of your uncle and he referred your name to me. I run a mining outfit in the frontier lands south of the city, and we believe we have found something of ancient magical import that may be of interest to you, as your uncle tells me that you wish to study such things.

But first, I have a job for you. I will be passing through Compton’s Crossing in three days time. If you are interested, you will be able to find me at the Driftwood Tavern and I will give you more details there.

I will be upfront. This job may carry some peril, so I would recommend bringing some of your heartiest friends along. This is no job for a single person. I am willing to pay twenty gold per individual.

Hope to meet you soon.

Regards,

Gundren

Hearty friends? Well, Katherine Yrendi is certainly hearty of mind but she is committed to a project at the Planar Studies Institute attempting to map the Astral Plane and is unavailable for this kind of field work. Well, there are always adventurers passing through town, perhaps you can recruit some to join you…
