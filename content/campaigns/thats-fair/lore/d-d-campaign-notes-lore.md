| Phandalin History | History | Once part of the Arganist empire. Phandalin was once a prosperous town in the Sword Coast North that, through the Phandelver's Pact — an alliance of humans, dwarves, and gnomes — gained access to the magical Forge of Spells in Wave Echo Cave. In 951 DR it was sacked by orcs and left abandoned for centuries. It was eventually resettled by farmers, woodcutters, fur traders, and prospectors drawn by stories of gold and platinum in the nearby Sword Mountains, becoming the modest frontier town it is in the late 15th century. |
